Purchase cost

Enter pre-VAT ₹/KL for receipt days so Analysis and Reports can calculate Nett Profit. Optional invoice no and supplier GSTIN feed the purchase GST register.

Receipt days need a purchase rate to calculate profit. Enter pre-VAT ₹/KL below (per 1000 L, as on the BPCL invoice). VAT/LST and delivery are applied in P&L. Each rate applies until the next receipt day. Selling rates come from the meter forms.

    All receipt days in the lookback window have a buying price. Full P&L is on Reports → Profit & Loss and Analysis.
